Smack Handsüñê (Roblox)

Smack Handsüñê is a game on Roblox that was inspired by two games named Slap Battlesüëè and Ability Wars.
Although the game can be played entirely for fun, the true objective is to obtain all hands.


In the game you can smack other players with hands for the game's currency; smacks. You get 1 smack every hit and you can unlock a variety of different hands with smacks. Another method of earning hands is via badges; which require different obtainment methods to unlock. The final method is gamepasses; which is a way to buy something (with Robux) and get it permanently on a game.

    Problem Solving and Community Support

    Situation

    The OP wanted a part to detect only an NPC instead of a player but could not figure out how to get it to work.

    Task

    Make the part detect the OP's NPC and not a player.

    Action

    I asked multiple questions to OP on whether he had some things missing (see posts 20+). He told me that those were correct, so I asked some more things to see if they were the issues and those were also correct.


    I then asked if it was the NPC that was the problem - so I told him to import a basic rig and see if that rig gets detected by the part; it did. The OP then wanted to know what was wrong with his NPC, so I investigated.


    I told OP it was because the HumanoidRootPart was anchored. Once unanchored, the OP's issue was resolved and I was given a solution.

    Result

    OP's NPC was detected by the part, and any players touching the part would be ignored.
